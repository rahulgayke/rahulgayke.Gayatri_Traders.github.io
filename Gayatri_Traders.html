Onion Specifications and Content

Specifications

  • Scientific name: Allium cepa
  • Family: Amaryllidaceae
  • Common names: Onion, bulb onion, common onion
  • Origin: Central Asia
  • Color: White, yellow, or red
  • Shape: Round or oblong
  • Size: Varies from 1-4 inches in diameter

Content

Onions are low in calories and high in nutrients. They are a good source of vitamin C, fiber, and various antioxidants. Onions also contain small amounts of other vitamins and minerals, such as vitamin B6, potassium, and folate.
