Product Specifications

Our raw onions have below specification in general:

  • Colors: Our Onions are available in Dark Red, Red and Light Red colors
  • Sizes: It ranges from 25mm to 80mm in Diameter
  • Origin: Central Asia
  • Optimum Relative Humidity: 25% to 30%
  • Shape: Round or oblong
  • Storage Life: Can be stored for as long as 5 to 6 months after harvesting
  • Type/Category: Available onion type is "Rabi Season Onion"

Product Contents

Fresh raw onions of 100 grams has following contents:

Onions are low in calories and high in nutrients. They are a good source of vitamin C, fiber, and various antioxidants. Onions also contain small amounts of other vitamins and minerals, such as vitamin B6, potassium, and folate.

Content by %weights:

  • Water: 89%
  • Calories: 40
  • Carbs: 9.3%
  • Fibre: 1.7%
  • Sugar: 4.2%
  • Protein: 1.1%
  • Fat: 0.1%
